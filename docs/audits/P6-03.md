# P6-03 Modern Taxonomy Review Audit

## Purpose

P6-03 performs the first evidence-backed taxonomy change after the frozen P6-01 j6e compatibility baseline. It compares the owner's recent-event classifications with the committed Modern archive, revises parent rules where the evidence supports a stable strategic identity, introduces approved subtypes, and freezes every resulting difference from P6-01.

Modern product activation remains outside this task.

## Approved taxonomy boundary

P6-03 uses a parent for a shared strategic engine and a subtype for a strongly evidenced color or construction branch within that engine.

The owner-requested boundaries are represented explicitly:

- `Broodscale Combo` has Gruul, Mono-Green, and Golgari subtypes.
- `Prowess` has Izzet, Temur, Grixis, Jeskai, and Lessons subtypes.
- `Eldrazi Tron` has Colorless, Mono-Green, and Mono-Black subtypes.
- Energy variants remain separate Boros, Mardu, Jeskai, Azorius, Selesnya, and Esper parent archetypes; they are not collapsed under an Energy parent.

Other evidence-backed subtype families include Blink, Blue-Black Tempo, Burn, Chant Control, Eldrazi Ramp, Goryo's Reanimator, Hammer Time, Hollow One, Necrodominance, Oculus Ritual, Persist Reanimator, Rhinos, Soultrader, and Steel-Cutter.

Sparse owner labels were reviewed but not converted into production rules merely because they appeared in the workbook. Hardened Scales, Kethis, and Valakut remain deferred examples because the compared sample did not establish sufficiently reliable representative signatures.

## Rule implementation

`my_archetypes/modern.yaml` now contains:

- 55 stable parent archetypes;
- 100 stable rule IDs;
- 54 subtype definitions;
- globally unique explicit priorities;
- explicit `main` scope on every card condition;
- no centroid or corpus-nearest-neighbor fallback;
- explicit `Unknown` for unmatched decks.

The taxonomy consolidates color variants where the owner requested a shared family, separates materially different strategies, and adds current strategies that were absent or hidden in the P6-01 Unknown population. Representative additions include Fight Rigging, Fling Goyf, Five-Color Creativity, Hammer Time, Hollow One, Rhinos, Soultrader, Song of Creation, and Pensive Combo.

## Compatibility and regression contracts

The original P6-01 rules are stored verbatim at `tests/fixtures/modern/p6_01_rules.yaml`. The original P6-01 migration tests now load that frozen fixture and continue to prove all 5,792 original parent results, the 38-rule source mapping, priority semantics, mainboard-only behavior, and absence of P6-01 subtypes.

`tests/fixtures/modern/taxonomy_contract.json` freezes the P6-03 result over the same de-identified corpus. It records:

- the P6-01 and P6-03 rule hashes and summary counts;
- exact selected parent counts;
- exact selected subtype counts;
- every P6-01-to-P6-03 parent transition;
- one synthetic representative for every selected parent/subtype identity and Unknown.

`tests/test_modern_taxonomy.py` verifies the complete production rule shape, the approved parent/subtype boundaries, the deliberate absence of low-evidence candidates, all frozen counts and transitions, representative cases, and rule-order independence.
