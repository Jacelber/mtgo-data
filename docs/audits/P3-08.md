## Front-end verification

The repository root was served over local HTTP and exercised in the in-app browser. The following behavior passed:

- the page and update metadata loaded with zero console errors or warnings;
- visible 1-, 4-, and 12-week statistics loaded;
- 4-week statistics showed 1,056 decks and 29 archetype rows;
- 12-week statistics showed 3,072 decks and 58 archetype rows;
- the Selesnya Offense deck-detail view showed the representative list, deviation, Core/Flex construction, and recent construction change;
- the 1-week matchup matrix showed 24 rows and the 12-week matrix showed 60 rows;
- `4-Color Tablet` remained present in both matchup scopes;
- Weekly Pickup loaded `2026-W27` and `4-Color Tablet`;
- temporarily removing the optional Pickup catalog produced the existing no-content fallback without breaking the page or logging an error;
- English and Chinese language switching remained functional.

The generated public catalogs include 36-week statistics and matchup documents, but `index.html` has intentionally exposed only 1, 4, and 12 weeks since before the Phase 2 recovery baseline because the 36-week view was considered insufficient for display. P3-08 corrects the smoke checklist to match that preserved behavior. Restoring the 36-week buttons remains a separate front-end product decision; `index.html` is unchanged.
